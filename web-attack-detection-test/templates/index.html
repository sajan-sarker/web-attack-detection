Overview

In today's highly digital and interconnected world, cybersecurity has become more critical than ever. One essential line of defense against cyber threats is the Intrusion Detection System (IDS), which monitors network traffic to detect unauthorized activities, anomalies, and malicious attacks. This project focuses on developing a highly accurate and efficient IDS using machine learning techniques, with the goal of automatically identifying both known and unknown cyberattacks in real-time. Leveraging the CIC-IDS2017 dataset, a comprehensive and realistic dataset developed by the Canadian Institute for Cybersecurity, the project simulates real-world network traffic including a wide range of modern attack types...
